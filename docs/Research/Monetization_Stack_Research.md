# Monetization Stack Research — SmartShopCA.ca

> Decision doc: Why these affiliate programs? What are the expected rates?

---

## Selected Programs

| Program | Type | Rate | Why Selected |
|:--|:--|:--|:--|
| **Best Buy Canada** | % of sale | 3–5% est. | Direct affiliate, stable, strong Canadian brand, wide product range |
| **Newegg Canada** | % of sale | Varies | Direct, good for tech/monitors, Canadian presence |
| **Canadian Tire** | % of sale | 3–5% est. | Good for Q2/Q3 outdoor/seasonal, trusted Canadian brand |
| **MaxBounty** | CPA per lead | $5–$100+ | Canadian HQ (Ottawa), 1,265+ offers, weekly payments, CPA model |
| **Financial product** | CPA per approval | $50–$200+ | Questrade, Scotiabank, credit cards — high value, low volume needed |
| **Amazon.ca** | % of sale | 1–2.5% on tech | Supplementary ONLY — never primary due to low rates and rate-cut history |
| **Own PDF guide** | 100% margin | $19–$29 | Fully owned, zero commission risk, builds brand |

---

## Revenue Math to Hit $2K/month

- 5 x financial product CPA at $100 = $500
- $3,000 in Best Buy/Newegg sales at avg 4% = $120
- 200 x email list PDF sales at $19 = $3,800 (over time, not month 1)
- Or: 1,500 email subscribers, 3% click-to-buy on seasonal campaign = ~$2K/month by month 6–9

**Assessment:** PLAUSIBLE — requires consistent email list growth and seasonal campaign execution.

---

## Why Not Other Options

- **Amazon-only:** Too risky — 1–2.5% on tech, history of overnight cuts
- **Display ads only (Mediavine/Raptive):** Requires 50K+ sessions/month — deferred to Phase 4
- **Sponsored content:** Requires established audience — not viable at launch
- **Dropshipping:** Completely different business model, not aligned with decision-helper philosophy
