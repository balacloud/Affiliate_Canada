# 🗺️ Master Plan: Canadian Seasonal Deals Engine

### "The Decision Helper for Canadians, Not Another Deal Spam Site"


***

## What We Are Building (One Sentence)

> A Canada-specific, quarterly seasonal buying-guide and deals site for the **Canadian remote worker / home-office niche**, designed to reduce decision fatigue, monetized via a diversified affiliate stack, structured to be cited by AI search engines, and grown via email — not SEO rankings.

***

## ✅ What We've Audited and Confirmed

Before the plan, here's what the audit framework says is REAL vs RISKY:


| Claim | Audit Tag | Evidence |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| SEO alone will drive traffic | 🔴 [MISLEADING] | Niche sites lost 65%+ traffic in 2024–2025 Google updates [^1][^2] |
| Amazon.ca is a safe primary affiliate | 🔴 [VERIFIED RISK] | Amazon slashed commissions overnight in 2020; computers/monitors pay only **1–2.5%** [^3][^4][^5] |
| FB ads → affiliate links directly | 🔴 [MISLEADING] | Violates FB ad policy; correct funnel is Ad → your page → email → affiliate [^6] |
| Email list is the real asset | ✅ [VERIFIED] | Email affiliate marketing drives 66%+ more conversions [^7] |
| MaxBounty is a legit Canadian CPA network | ✅ [VERIFIED] | Ottawa-based, 1,265+ offers, weekly payments, well-reviewed [^8] |
| AI-structured content = Day 1 traffic | 🟡 [PLAUSIBLE] | Medium-term moat only; new domains need 6–12 months of authority-building [^9][^10] |
| Behavioral "less choice" design converts better | ✅ [VERIFIED] | Psychological research on choice overload + decision fatigue [^11][^12][^13] |


***

## 🏗️ Architecture in One Page

```
[FB Ad] 
   ↓
[Landing Page — your domain — seasonal hook]
   ↓
[Email Capture — lead magnet: "Canadian WFH Setup Guide PDF"]
   ↓
[Email Sequence — ConvertKit (3–5 emails, opinionated picks, affiliate links)]
   ↓
[Site — Decision Helper Pages — 3 picks per scenario, pros/cons, schema markup]
   ↓
[Affiliate Programs — Best Buy CA, Newegg, Canadian Tire, MaxBounty, 1 financial product]
   ↓
[Email List grows → seasonal campaigns each quarter → repeat revenue]
```

SEO and AI search citations build slowly on top of this. Email is the spine.[^7][^14]

**Primary KPI:** First affiliate commissions earned (even \$50 is a real signal)

### Phase 3 — Weeks 15–22 (Q4 Boxing Day Super Season)

## 🗒️ Notion Checklist: Pre-Publish (AI-Era)

Paste this as a Notion database template, one entry per page:

- [ ] Title is a specific question with "Canada" and year in it
- [ ] TL;DR summary (2–4 bullets) at top of page
- [ ] Max 7 product picks (3 is ideal)
- [ ] Every product has: name, merchant, CAD price, 3 pros, 2 cons, "Choose this if…" sentence
- [ ] JSON-LD schema: Product + ItemList + FAQPage added
- [ ] "In Canada" / CAD explicit in intro
- [ ] Seasonal tag visible (e.g., "Q2 2026 Spring Picks")
- [ ] FAQ block: minimum 3 questions
- [ ] Internal links to ≥1 related page
- [ ] "Last updated [Month Year]" visible on page
- [ ] Email capture CTA at bottom
- [ ] All affiliate links go through OutboundTracker component

## 🚨 The 5 Rules Codex Must Never Break

1. **Never link FB ads directly to affiliate/merchant URLs** — always to your own domain[^6]
2. **Amazon.ca is supplementary only** — never the primary CTA on any page[^3][^4][^5]
3. **Maximum 7 products per collection page** — behavioral design rule, never a catalog[^11][^20]
4. **Every page must have schema markup** — non-negotiable for AI search visibility[^21][^22]
5. **Email capture on every page** — the list is the real business, the site is the acquisition tool[^14][^7]
