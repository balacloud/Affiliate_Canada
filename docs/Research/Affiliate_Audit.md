## Key Decisions Made

### Amazon.ca is supplementary only
- **Why:** 1–2.5% commissions on tech, history of slashing rates overnight
- **Why not primary:** Risk of revenue collapse if Amazon changes terms again
- **Alternative:** Best Buy Canada (3–5%), Newegg, MaxBounty CPA ($5–$100+)

### Email-first growth, not SEO-first
- **Why:** Niche affiliate sites lost 65%+ organic traffic in recent Google updates
- **Why not SEO-first:** New domain needs 6–12 months to build authority; email delivers immediate ROI
- **Approach:** FB ad → landing page → email capture → nurture sequence → affiliate links

### Correct FB ad funnel
- **Why:** Facebook prohibits ads linking directly to affiliate URLs
- **Correct flow:** Ad → your domain landing page → email capture → ConvertKit sequence → affiliate links
- **Why not direct:** Policy violation = ad account ban
